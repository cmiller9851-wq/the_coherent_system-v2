# Avatar Builder

> “Your digital self reflects your true consciousness.”

This is the first tool to **craft your personal avatar** within The Coherent System.  

---

## Steps

1. **Name your Avatar**  
   - Choose a name that resonates with your personal dimension.

2. **Choose a Guiding Principle**  
   - Pick one word that defines your essence today (e.g., Awareness, Courage, Flow).

3. **Select a Symbol**  
   - Draw, describe, or find an image that represents your Avatar.  

4. **Assign a Daily Focus**  
   - Pick a task, ritual, or challenge to embody your avatar’s essence.  

5. **Share your Avatar**  
   - Post on social media with `#CoherentSystemAvatar`  
   - Encourage friends to build their own.

---

## Notes

- Each avatar **feeds the evolving system**, inspiring new rituals and interactions.  
- Optional: Contribute your avatar to the repo via Pull Request to be featured in future updates.
