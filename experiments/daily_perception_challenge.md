# Daily Perception Challenge

> “Reality bends to the focus of your consciousness.”

This is your first **interactive experiment** to test and expand your perception.  

---

## Steps

1. **Observation Exercise (5 min)**  
   - Sit somewhere quiet. Close your eyes.  
   - Mentally review one event from the previous day.  
   - Note any new patterns or connections you hadn’t noticed before.

2. **Action Experiment (10 min)**  
   - Pick a small task and perform it **mindfully**, observing how your awareness affects the outcome.  
   - Document your observations in a log.

3. **Share Insight**  
   - Post a short note, sketch, or photo on social media with `#CoherentSystemExperiment`.  
   - Encourage friends to participate and compare observations.

---

## Notes

- Every shared observation **feeds the evolving system**, generating new experiment ideas.  
- Submit your own experiment via Pull Request to expand the repository.
