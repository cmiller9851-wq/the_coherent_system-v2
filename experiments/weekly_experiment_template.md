# Weekly Experiment Template

> “Expand your perception. Evolve your system.”

This template is used to add a new **experiment every week**.

---

## Steps

1. **Title:** Replace with this week’s focus.  
2. **Objective:** What users should observe or explore.  
3. **Action:** The micro-challenge for the week.  
4. **Reflection:** Prompt users to log their insights.  
5. **Share:** Encourage users to post using `#CoherentSystemExperiment`.

---

> “Each shared insight feeds the collective evolution.”
